Tactical Read

Watch for three-wave bounces into resistance rather than assuming the first green session repairs the tape. The best bullish evidence would be impulsive bounces with volatility fading and the dollar failing. The best bearish evidence would be rallies into resistance while VIX holds firm and cyclical groups keep making lower lows.

The index map keeps Dow, QQQ, and Russell on alert. Dow has lower supports near the 50,100 / 50,000 region and then 48,747. QQQ can bounce near 700 and still remain vulnerable to a deeper move. Russell's 270 region is important for whether speculative breadth can hold.

Gold and silver may still need lower support boxes before the next cleaner long setup. Oil is especially important: weakness below its key shelf can become a demand-destruction signal, while a reclaim can produce a retest and temporary inflation impulse.
